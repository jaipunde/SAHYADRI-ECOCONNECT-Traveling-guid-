About This ExperienceExperience the magic

 We’re headed to Bhandardara, a picturesque village nestled in the heart of the KalsubaiHarishchandragadh Wildlife Reserve. Every year, just before the downpour of the monsoon arrives, these forests come alive in a truly magical phenomenon—millions and millions of fireflies come out of the trees looking for their mates. Imagine the whole forest filled with countless tiny fairy lights; it really is a once-in-a-lifetime experience. Our campsite is situated on the bank of the lake overlooking the magnificent Lake Arthur. Come join us, and let us take you to ‘Where The Light Is’! 

Local CuisineTaste the tradition

 Maharashtrian cuisine is a vibrant blend of mild and spicy dishes, featuring staples like wheat, rice, jowar, bajri, vegetables, lentils, and fruits. It's known for its unique spice mixes and a variety of dishes, from the sweet Modak to the savory Puran Poli

Day 1

Embark on a journey to Bhandardara in rural Maharashtra, where you'll experience the tranquility of Arthur Lake and the serene beauty of a sunset swim. After a hearty dinner of local cuisine, enjoy the camaraderie around the campfire, followed by a mesmerizing encounter with fireflies in the deep forest. This adventure promises a blend of relaxation, excitement, and a unique opportunity to connect with nature, making it an unforgettable experience.
